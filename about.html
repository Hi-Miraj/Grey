About Me

I am the co-founder & CFO of MedVisual, an AI-powered healthcare startup transforming medical report visualization and accessibility. My work focuses on bridging healthcare gaps through AI-driven solutions.

My journey began with robotics, where I started coding with Arduino and later transitioned to Python to explore AI and computer vision. This passion led me to hackathons, where I've won national and international awards, pushing the boundaries of healthtech and automation.

Always eager to innovate, I continue exploring AI, machine learning, and robotics to solve real-world challenges.
